Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies?

The home must either be owned outright or under mortgage in the signer’s name. No renters or leased homes qualify.

Are there deposits or credit checks?

No credit checks and no deposits.

Does this include free nights or solar buyback?

No. This is a separate electricity program that uses a participating REP that supports the VPP battery network.

How long is the term?

The VPP electricity agreement is a 36‑month term.
