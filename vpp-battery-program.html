Important program details

Does this include solar buyback or free nights?

No. This is a separate VPP energy plan. Free nights and solar buyback can be offered as different residential plans, but they are not part of this battery program.

How long is the plan?

Homeowners who enroll move to a participating REP that supports the VPP program for a 36-month term.

Who qualifies?

The signer must own the home outright or have the mortgage under their name.

Do renters qualify?

No. Apartments, renters, leased homes, and non-owner situations do not qualify.
