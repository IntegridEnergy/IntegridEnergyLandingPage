Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Integrid Energy LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you visit our website, submit a quote request, or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address, electricity usage information, current provider information, and details you submit through forms or direct communication.

Analytics and Tracking

Our website may use tools such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, HubSpot tracking, and similar technologies to understand website traffic, measure performance, improve marketing, and follow up with interested visitors.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to provide electricity quotes, perform cost analysis, communicate with customers, improve our services, and help match customers with available electricity options.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with electricity suppliers, retail electric providers, or service partners only when necessary to obtain pricing, prepare contracts, or provide requested services.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to improve site performance, measure advertising effectiveness, and understand visitor behavior.
