HubSpot Integration Instructions

This website package is ready for HubSpot tracking.

What you need:
1. Log in to HubSpot.
2. Go to Settings > Tracking & Analytics > Tracking Code.
3. Copy your HubSpot portal ID or tracking script.
4. Open each HTML file and replace:
   YOUR_HUBSPOT_PORTAL_ID
   with your actual HubSpot portal ID.

Example:
<script type="text/javascript" id="hs-script-loader" async defer src="//js.hs-scripts.com/12345678.js"></script>

After replacing it:
- Upload all files to GitHub.
- Cloudflare will redeploy automatically.
- HubSpot will start tracking page visits.
- HubSpot forms/chat can be added next.
